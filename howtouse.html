How to use Javier Voice Assistant


Speak clearly after the tone.

Commands:

  • Wikipedia
  • browse the internet
  • search for the news
  • tell a joke
  • tell the time
  • tell the date

Note: You don't have to tell these exact commands. Just include the action you want Javiar to perform.

Note: To close it, say "shut down" or "sleep".

Known bugs: Sometimes, when searching Wikipedia, it'll crash.
